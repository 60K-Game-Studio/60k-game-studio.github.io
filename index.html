60K Game Studio

60K Game Studio is an independent game development team founded by passionate creators who believe that every great experience begins with bold ideas and solid craftsmanship. We focus on building original gameplay systems, expressive characters, and visually captivating worlds — all powered by our custom-built frameworks and artistic direction.

Our goal is to create games that feel alive, with deep mechanics, unique personality, and unforgettable moments. From code architecture and animation systems to shaders and storytelling, everything we build is driven by creativity, precision, and heart.
